French Roast


French roast is a bold and robust coffee variety known for its deep, rich flavor profile. This dark roast is achieved by allowing the coffee beans to roast for an extended period, resulting in a glossy, oily surface and a distinct smoky flavor. The intense heat transforms the beans, giving them a dark brown to nearly black color and a crisp, shiny appearance. French roast coffee is characterized by its low acidity, full-bodied texture, and prominent notes of bittersweet chocolate and caramel. This roast is popular among coffee enthusiasts who appreciate a strong and hearty cup, making it an excellent choice for those who enjoy a more intense and complex coffee experience.

Columbian Coffee


Colombian coffee is renowned worldwide for its exceptional quality and distinctively smooth taste. Grown in the high-altitude regions of Colombia's Andes Mountains, the coffee beans benefit from the unique combination of rich volcanic soil, ample rainfall, and ideal climatic conditions. Colombian coffee is often characterized by its medium body, balanced acidity, and a well-rounded flavor profile. With hints of nuttiness, a touch of citrus, and a mild sweetness, Colombian coffee is widely celebrated for its versatility and broad appeal. The country's commitment to quality and stringent grading standards contribute to the consistently high caliber of Colombian coffee, making it a beloved choice for those seeking a delightful and approachable cup of coffee.
